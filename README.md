## About

VitaQ is a web-based community clinic appointment and queue system for public health clinics in South Africa.

It helps patients book appointments, join queues remotely, and track their status in real time, while giving clinic staff tools to manage patient flow more efficiently.

## Features

- Patient registration and login
- Clinic search and discovery
- Appointment slot viewing and booking
- Virtual queue tracking
- Notifications and reminders
- Clinic and staff management
- Analytics and reporting
