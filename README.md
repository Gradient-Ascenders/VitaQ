# 🏥 VitaQ

**VitaQ** is a web-based **Community Clinic Appointment & Queue System** designed to improve patient flow in public health clinics. It helps patients book appointments, join virtual queues remotely, and receive real-time updates, while giving clinic staff tools to manage queues and appointments more efficiently.

## Problem

Public health clinics across South Africa are often overcrowded, with patients waiting for hours and having little visibility into their place in the queue. This leads to frustration, wasted time, and in some cases patients leaving before receiving care.

VitaQ aims to solve this by creating a digital system that improves accessibility, transparency, and clinic operations.

## Project Goals

- Reduce physical waiting times at community clinics
- Allow patients to book appointments online
- Enable patients to join and monitor virtual queues
- Give clinic staff tools to manage patient flow
- Provide notifications and reminders
- Support clinic data management and analytics

## Core Features

- **User registration and login**
- **Clinic search and discovery**
- **Appointment slot viewing**
- **Appointment booking**
- **Virtual queue management**
- **Real-time queue status tracking**
- **Notifications and reminders**
- **Clinic and staff management**
- **Analytics and reporting**

## Sprint Plan

### Sprint 1 — Core System & Booking
- Register and login securely
- Search for nearby clinics
- View available appointment slots
- Book appointments

### Sprint 2 — Queue Management
- Join a virtual queue
- Staff queue management
- Update patient status
- View live queue position
- Receive queue notifications
- Add walk-in patients

### Sprint 3 — Data Integration & Management
- Integrate South African clinic datasets
- Filter clinics by service and region
- Manage clinic details
- Assign staff to clinics
- Set staff availability
- Reschedule or cancel appointments

### Sprint 4 — Analytics & Refinement
- View wait time analytics
- Track no-show rates
- Export reports

## Target Users

### Patients
Patients use VitaQ to:
- Create accounts and log in securely
- Find nearby clinics
- Book appointments
- Join queues remotely
- Track their queue position
- Receive updates and reminders

### Clinic Staff
Clinic staff use VitaQ to:
- Manage queues
- Update patient progress/status
- Handle walk-ins
- Monitor daily patient flow

### Admins
Admins use VitaQ to:
- Maintain clinic and staff information
- Monitor system performance
- Access analytics and reports
